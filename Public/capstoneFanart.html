HKMO Fan Art Competition 2018

The annual fan art competition will be held on 28th August. Till then, you can submit your fan art on social media to support the local bands and participate in the lucky draw.

Winners of this competition will receive a two-way ticket from Hong Kong to Las Vegas as the grand prize!

  • Disclaimer
  • 1. Copyright images produced will be owned by HKMO
  • 2. No plagiarism allowed
  • 3. Artwork must be submitted before 26th August 2018
  • 4. The score given by the panel of judges will not be overwritten
  • 5. HKMO reserve the right to overrule any decision in the competition
